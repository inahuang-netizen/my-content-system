# Skill: Learn

## Purpose
Capture, organize, and surface new learnings, insights, or knowledge gained during work sessions.

## Usage
Invoke this skill to log something new you've learned or to retrieve past learnings.

## What to Log
- New facts, concepts, or frameworks discovered
- Mistakes made and what was learned from them
- Useful tools, techniques, or references found
- Feedback received and applied

## Output Format
When logging a learning:
1. **What**: The key fact or insight
2. **Why it matters**: Relevance to the project or work
3. **Source**: Where it came from (optional)
4. **Date**: When it was logged

## Storage
Learnings are appended to two files:
- `memory.md` — under a `## Learnings` section (persistent knowledge base)
- `output.md` — as a dated entry (running session output log)

When logging, append the entry to both files.

## Example Invocation
> /learn I discovered that X approach works better than Y for this use case because...
