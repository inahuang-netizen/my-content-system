# Skill: Tone Checker

## Purpose
Review content for tone, style, and voice consistency. Identify issues and suggest improvements.

## Usage
Invoke this skill when you want feedback on the tone of a piece of content.

## What to Check
- **Voice**: Is it consistent throughout? Active vs. passive?
- **Formality**: Does the register match the intended audience?
- **Clarity**: Is the message clear and direct?
- **Empathy**: Does it connect with the reader appropriately?
- **Brand alignment**: Does it match the intended brand voice?

## Output Format
1. Overall tone assessment (1-2 sentences)
2. Specific issues found (bulleted list)
3. Suggested rewrites for flagged sections
4. Final verdict: Approve / Revise

## Example Invocation
> /tone-checker [paste content here]
